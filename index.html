Privacy Policy for Addressclip Chrome Extension

Addressclip does not collect personal information such as names, emails, passwords, or financial data. The extension only collects public wallet addresses and optional affiliate codes when the user initiates a rug check on Axiom.trade. This information is securely transmitted over HTTPS and Base64 encoded.

Addressclip does not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties except to process rug pull analysis as described. Data collected is used solely to provide the rug pull risk score and trading tools.
