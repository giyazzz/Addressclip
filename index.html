Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 8, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles data. By using our extension, you agree to the practices described here.

Data Collection

Our extension does not collect personal information. The only data accessed is necessary for the extension’s core functionality:

  • Active Tab Content: To read blockchain wallet addresses and token contract information from pages you visit.
  • Local Storage: To save user preferences and copied addresses locally in your browser.

Data Usage

The data accessed by the extension is used solely to provide its features. No information is sent to any server or third-party service without your explicit action.

Permissions

The extension requests only the following permissions for its functionality:

  • activeTab – To access the content of the current page for copying addresses.
  • tabs – To identify and interact with the active tab.
  • storage – To save user preferences locally.
  • Host permissions – Limited to pages where blockchain addresses or token information appear.
